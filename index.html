Who built this

Ankit Musaddi
Founder

CA · AI Systems Architect · West Bengal, India

LedgerFlow was founded on a simple observation: the tools to automate finance exist, the AI to power them is ready, but almost nobody with real finance expertise is building with them.

Ankit Musaddi is a Chartered Accountant with a decade of operational business experience — running a family rice processing company taught him what financial pain really looks like from the inside.

After returning to CA practice in 2025, he combined that operational instinct with Claude AI, n8n automation, and modern APIs to build systems that replace the manual work most finance teams do every day.

LedgerFlow is the result: a self-evolving intelligent portfolio that converts every system built into a client-ready proof of capability — automatically.
